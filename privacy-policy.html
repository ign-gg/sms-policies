Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how this SMS notification service ("the Service") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with text messages sent and received through it.

Who operates this service

This Service is operated by an individual (the "Operator") for personal, non-commercial use. It sends automated text message alerts from a personal home server and a personal Discord bot to the Operator's own phone number.

Information we collect

The only information associated with this messaging program is the phone number used to send and receive alerts, and the content of the alert messages themselves (for example, server event notifications or Discord support ticket notifications). No additional personal information is collected through this messaging program.

No mobile opt-in data is shared or sold. All categories of information collected through this messaging program — including text messaging originator opt-in data and consent — are never shared, sold, rented, or disclosed to any third party, affiliate, or lead generator for marketing, promotional, or any other purposes, at any time.

How information is used

The phone number on file is used solely to deliver automated alert messages related to this Service (such as home server monitoring events and Discord support ticket notifications) and to process opt-out (STOP) and help (HELP) requests.

Data sharing

No information collected through this messaging program is shared with or sold to third parties. The Service does not use messaging data for advertising or marketing purposes of any kind.

Message frequency and opt-out

Message frequency varies based on triggered events. Message and data rates may apply, depending on your mobile carrier plan. You may opt out of messages at any time by replying STOP. You may request help by replying HELP.

Data retention

Message logs are retained only as long as necessary to operate and troubleshoot the Service, and are not used for any purpose beyond delivering and confirming alert messages.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be directed to the Operator via the contact information provided in the associated Twilio account registration.
